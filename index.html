Purpose of Study

This research study is exploring the effects of watching pornography within a relationship. It is open to anyone in a relationship (longer than 4 months) where either member (or both) has watched any pornography in the last month. It involves having each member of a couple describe some aspects of their relationship and report about both their usage and their partner's usage of pornography. All information collected will be anonymous and is used only for academic research purposes. The survey phase of this study will run from May 25, 2019 through July 31, 2019.
