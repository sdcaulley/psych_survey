Purpose of Study

This research study is exploring the effects of watching pornography within a relationship. It consists of a questionnaire where you will fill in information about your sexual relationship and sexual experiences. It is open to anyone in a relationship (longer than 4 months) where either member has watched any pornography in the last month.

The survey should take about 25 minutes on average to complete. All information collected will be anonymous and is used only for academic research purposes. If you wish to withdraw from the study, you can do so at any moment without any negative consequences and without having to give any reason. The survey phase of this study is planned to run from June 15th, 2019 through July 31, 2019.
